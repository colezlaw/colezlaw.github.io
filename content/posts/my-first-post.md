## Ahoy!

In 2022, one of the local football club blogs was having a video podcast, and
in the live comments somebody on a mobile phone said "Westwood should ahoy more"
which I assume was "shoot". But "ahoy" stuck as a word we yell when somebody
shoots from outside the box now.
